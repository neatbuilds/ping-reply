# Never miss a chance to reply

**PingReply** automatically sends an SMS when you miss a call. The caller knows you saw it. You follow up when you're ready.

[Get it on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.callreplyfilter.app){: .btn .btn--primary .btn--large}

---

## How it works

Set up in seconds. No accounts, no registration.

1. **Write your message** — Type the auto-reply you want callers to receive.
2. **Turn it on** — Flip the toggle. PingReply runs quietly in the background.
3. **Miss a call** — Your custom SMS is sent automatically. Done.

## Permissions explained

PingReply needs these permissions to work, and nothing else:

| Permission | Why it is needed |
|---|---|
| **Phone State** | Detect when an incoming call is missed |
| **Call Log** | Required by Android 9+ to get the caller's phone number |
| **Send SMS** | Send your auto-reply to the caller |
| **Contacts** | Show contact names in the exclusion list |
| **Notifications** | Show a persistent notification while the service is active |

These permissions are used exclusively for the app's core function. No data from these permissions is stored, collected, or transmitted.
