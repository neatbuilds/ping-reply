## How the Application Works

PingReply automatically sends a pre-written SMS message when you miss a phone call. The Application detects missed calls on your device and sends your custom text message to the caller's number. All processing happens locally on your device.

The Application does not require registration, does not create user accounts, and does not connect to any servers. No personal data is transmitted from your device at any time during the normal operation of the Application.

---

## Permissions and Why They Are Needed

The Application requires access to the following device features. Each is used strictly for the Application's core purpose and nothing else.

| Permission | Why it is needed |
|---|---|
| `READ_PHONE_STATE` | To detect when an incoming call is missed by monitoring call state changes from ringing to idle. |
| `READ_CALL_LOG` | Required by Android 9 and above to receive the caller's phone number when a call state change occurs. |
| `SEND_SMS` | To send the auto-reply text message to the caller's number after a missed call is detected. |
| `READ_CONTACTS` | To display contact names in the exclusion list. No contact data is stored or transmitted. |
| `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` | To show a persistent notification indicating the service is active (required by Android 13+). |

The Application does not use the INTERNET permission. No data obtained through the above permissions is collected, stored on any server, or transmitted anywhere.

## Personal Data We Collect and Process

**The Application does not collect or process personal data.** No user accounts exist, no registration is required, and no data is transmitted from your device.

The only data stored locally on your device is:

- Your auto-reply message text
- Your on/off toggle and service preferences
- Excluded phone numbers list (numbers that should not receive auto-replies)
- History of missed calls and sent replies
- Daily and monthly SMS usage counters

This data is stored exclusively on your device, is not accessible to us, and is permanently deleted when you uninstall the Application.
